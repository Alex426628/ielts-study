Part 1 · 2 · 3 뼈대
어떤 주제가 나와도 쓸 수 있는 파트별 답변 구조 + 실전 표현 모음
파트별 뼈대
PART 1 일상 Q&A 4–5분
뼈대 구조 · 2–3문장
1
직접 답하기
"I'd say I'm pretty much into…"
질문에 바로 답. Yes/No로 끝내지 말 것.
2
이유 하나 추가
"…mainly because…"
이유 1개면 충분. 2개 이상은 Part 3에서.
3
가볍게 마무리
"…so it's kind of become a big part of my life."
짧고 자연스럽게 닫기.
실전 예시

"I'd say I'm pretty much into following global markets, mainly because I find it genuinely exciting to track how things move. So yeah, it's kind of become a big part of my daily routine."

PART 2 Cue Card 독백 3–4분
1분 메모 → 2분 독백
1
오프닝
"I'd like to talk about…"
WHEN + WHERE + WHO를 1문장에 압축.
2
핵심 설명
"What I particularly liked about it was…"
감각 묘사 추가 → 시간 자연스럽게 채우기.
3
감정 분리
"At the time I felt… Looking back now, I think…"
같은 주제를 두 각도로 → 30초 추가.
4
마무리
"That's why it's so memorable to me."
WHY로 닫기. 새 정보 금지.
실전 예시

"I'd like to talk about a trip I took to Jeju last July. What I particularly liked was hiking Hallasan at dawn — the air was cold and the trail was steep. At the time I felt exhausted, but looking back now I think it was one of the best decisions I've made. That's why it's still so memorable."

PART 3 추상 토론 4–5분
PEEL · 최소 5문장
P
Point — 의견
"I would argue that…" / "From my perspective…"
E
Explain — 이유
"This is mainly because…" / "The reason is that…"
E
Example — 사례
"Take, for example…" / "Consider the case of…"
L
Link — 마무리
"So yes, that's why I think…"
원래 질문으로 연결하며 닫기.
실전 예시

"I would argue that remote work has fundamentally changed how people think about careers. This is mainly because it removed the need to live near your workplace. Take, for example, how many Koreans now work for global companies without relocating. So yes, that's why I think geography matters much less than it used to."

시간 버는 표현
Filler Phrases 모음
어떤 주제에도 사용 가능
의견 시작 · Part 1 · 3
I'd say… I would argue that… From my perspective… To be honest with you… It seems to me that…
시간 버는 표현 · 막혔을 때
That's an interesting question. Let me think about that for a moment. It depends on how you look at it. Well, in a sense… It's hard to say, but I'd guess…
예시 도입 · Part 2 · 3
Take, for example… A good illustration of this is… Consider the case of… For instance…
반론 인정 · Part 3 Band 7 필수
Having said that… Then again… I can see why some people… Admittedly…
💡
핵심 포인트: 뼈대를 외우면 주제가 뭐가 나와도 첫 5초 안에 구조가 잡힙니다. Part 1은 2–3문장, Part 2는 감정 분리로 2분, Part 3는 PEEL 5문장 이상이 목표입니다.
